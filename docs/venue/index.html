Venue

ACM Multimedia Asia 2022 will be a hybrid conference with both in-person and online attendance options.

We will release the information on online attendance soon.


Yayoi Auditorium


The conference will be held in Tokyo, the safest, most attractive, and most convenient city located in Japan. Our venue, the Yayoi Auditorium at The University of Tokyo, is in the heart of the city and is easily reachable from all over the world.


Access

Subway

  • 1-minute walk from Todaimae station (東大前駅), Tokyo Metro Namboku Line (東京メトロ南北線)
  • 8-minute walk from Nezu station (根津駅), Tokyo Metro Chiyoda Line (東京メトロ千代田線)

Bus

From Ochanomizu station, take the Toei bus (都営バス) bound for either:

  • Komagome Sta. Minamiguchi (茶51駒込駅南口), or
  • Arakawadote-Soshajo (東43荒川土手操車所前).

Get off the bus at Todai-Nogakubu (東大農学部前). 1-minute walk from the bus stop.
